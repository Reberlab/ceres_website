Cognitive Expertise through Repetition Enhanced Simulation-based training (CERES)

Types of Training Tasks

Coordinate Training Task

The purpose of this task is to train on finding coordinates on a map using the Military Grid Reference System (MGRS).

Users have to mark on the map where they believe the correct location of the given coordinates. They have access to a protractor, which can help them find the answer. Points are credited based on accuracy.

Quantico Demonstration

Azimuth Training Task

The purpose of this task is to train how to find the azimuth between two points.

Users must find two point and then input the degree (increments of 5) that they believe the two point are from one another. Users have access to a protractor to help them find the coordinate points and the degrees, as well as a tool to draw a straight line between the points. Points are credited based on accuracy of marking the two given coordinates and inputting the correct azimuth.

Quantico Demonstration

Terrain Association Task

The purpose of this task is to train users how to determine where they are located on a map using the terrain and landmarks around them.

Users are presented a video and must use the information that they see in that video to try to determine where they are located on a map. Points are credited based on how close they mark their location to the correct answer and on the direction they mark that they are facing.
