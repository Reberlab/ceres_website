How to Submit an Answer:

  • 1. Select the “Answer” button under the map
  • 2. Click within the blue circle to mark the location
  • 3. Drag the arrow in the direction shown at the start of the video
  • 4. Click the “Answer” button a second time to finalize your response
  • 5. A green arrow will appear showing the correct answer. You will be awarded points for accuracy. The key to success in CERES is to be as precise as possible in your location answer.
